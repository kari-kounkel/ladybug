The Book

Dignity and delight were
never supposed to be enemies.

This one's for every woman — the married, the single, and the ones stuck somewhere in the confusing middle she lovingly calls The Halfsies.

There is room here for women who are thriving. Women who are rebuilding. Women who are exhausted, healing, becoming. Women who wear pearls and women who keep glue sticks, bobby pins, and cough drops floating in the bottom of their purse like a survival kit from a minor apocalypse.

You belong here too.

Thirteen chapters. Thirteen flowers. Thirteen verses. And a thirteenth chapter that leaves the page open — because this was never a performance you watched from the audience. It's a table you joined.
